Messages

Configuration

Outgoing messages can be configured to include data from the document by wrapping the field name in braces, eg:

Thank you {~lb}{~lb}contact.name{~rb}{~rb}, {~lb}{~lb}patient_name{~rb}{~rb} has been registered.

More information.

Available Fields

contact
The contact at the clinic. Use contact.name or contact.phone.
clinic
The clinic
parent
The parent facility of the clinic
grandparent
The grandparent facility of the clinic
health_center
The health center
district
The district
All other fields on the document are included

Formatters

To format dates using the configured date format:

Due date: {~lb}{~lb}#datetime{~rb}{~rb}{~lb}{~lb}expected_date{~rb}{~rb}{~lb}{~lb}/datetime{~rb}{~rb}
